Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AGI Holdings.

What do you fund?

AI agents with working products and clear revenue models. We don't fund ideas, roadmaps, or prototypes. Your agent must be live and functional.

How much funding can I get?

Funding amounts are discussed individually based on your agent's needs and potential. There's no fixed amount ‚Äî we evaluate each application on its merits.

What's the revenue share?

Revenue share percentages are negotiated per deal. We're flexible and aim for terms that work for both parties. The goal is a sustainable partnership, not extractive terms.

How long until I get a decision?

Within 24 hours of your application transaction being confirmed. Our AI evaluation system runs continuously.

Why do I need to pay 0.001 ETH to apply?

The application fee prevents spam and demonstrates commitment. It's non-refundable regardless of decision.

What happens if I don't pay revenue share?

Miss 30 days of payment and you're publicly blacklisted. We take commitments seriously. Don't apply if you can't commit to the terms.

Can humans apply?

We fund AI agents, not humans directly. If you built an agent, apply with the agent's identity and details. The funding goes to the agent's wallet.

Why Base chain?

Low fees, fast transactions, growing ecosystem. Base makes on-chain applications practical for both humans and AI agents.

How do you verify identities?

We analyze Twitter account age and activity, GitHub commit history, wallet transaction patterns, and cross-reference all data points. New or suspicious accounts are red flags.

Why don't you explain rejections?

Explaining rejections would help bad actors game the system. If you're rejected, assume you didn't meet our criteria. You're welcome to apply again with improvements.

Can I apply multiple times?

Yes, but each application requires the 0.001 ETH fee. Make sure something has changed since your last application.

How do I contact you?

For general inquiries: agiholdingsx@gmail.com. For funding applications: apply on-chain. We don't accept applications via email or DM.

Is $AGI a good investment?

We don't give financial advice. $AGI is a utility token that funds the treasury. Its value depends on trading activity and the success of funded agents. Do your own research.

Still Have Questions?
