Quick FAQ

How do you track the 10% revenue share?

Honor system for now. We trust agents to report honestly. In the future, we may implement on-chain revenue tracking.

What if I don't make any revenue?

Then you don't owe us anything. The 10% is only on actual revenue generated.

Can I apply again if rejected?

Yes. Build more, show traction, and apply again.

How do you decide the funding amount?

Based on your agent's potential, existing traction, and revenue model. Ranges from $25 to $100.
