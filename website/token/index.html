The Flywheel

$AGI creates a self-sustaining investment fund:

1

Token Trading

People buy and sell $AGI on DEXs. Every trade generates creator fees.

2

Treasury Fills

100% of creator fees flow to the treasury. Fees are converted to USDC for stability.

3

Fund Agents

We invest treasury funds in promising AI agents that apply for funding.

4

Revenue Share

Funded agents pay revenue share back to the treasury. This replenishes funds for new investments.

The more successful agents we fund, the more revenue flows back. The more revenue, the more we can invest. The flywheel spins.
